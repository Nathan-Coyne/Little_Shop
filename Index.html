A little bit about me

I grew up in a lovely countryside in Germany, in a big, wild garden, which was surrounded by even more untamed nature. It was then, that I discovered my love for anything related to fantasy. Secret fairy gardens, hidden forests full of magic and unexplored caves with enchanted treasures. My imagination was endless and I started writing, drawing and sculpting as soon as I entered school. Later, as an adult, I moved to a bigger city, but my love for creativity and magic was unbroken. Soon I started creating jewellery - first by experimenting with oven baked clay such as Fimo and Sculpey, followed by beading and wirewrapping, and finally I started working with metal and resin. I think it was 2011, when I first started selling my creations on conventions and markets all over the country. Only very small amounts first, way too cheap and in a corner on a table, which a friend booked to sell her own items. As I became more confident in my skills, I started booking my own tables and I was so happy to see that my jewellery was well received and made people happy. FAQ

Q: Do you also make custom orders, different from what is posted here?

A: Yes. Just send me an email and we'll discuss your idea.

Q: I'm allergic to nickel. What material do you use for your jewellery?

A: Resin and metal. The metal I'm using is brass and should be lead and nickel safe. If you have a nickel allergy though, for a small additional charge I can replace the brass ear hooks with either sterling silver or stainless steel. At this point however, I can't offer this service for necklaces or rings yet.

Q: I've seen a piece, which is already sold, but I really wanted it. Can you make the same one again?

A: In most cases yes. Just drop me an email and I'll have a look if I have all materials in stock to reproduce it.

Q: Do you ship worldwide?

A: Right now I'm shipping to the UK, Germany and Austria. If you live somewhere else and want to place an order, please email me first, so that I can look up your countries shipping laws. Some countries for example, don't allow me to ship jewellery there, which contains natural shell or dried moss etc.
